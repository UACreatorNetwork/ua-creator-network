Here are the basics before applying to UA Creator Network.

What is UA Creator Network?

UA Creator Network is a creator-first TikTok LIVE community built around support, growth, connection, and a no-pressure culture.

Who can apply?

Creators aged 18+ who want to grow on TikTok LIVE and be part of a supportive community can apply.

Do I have to battle?

No. Battles are optional. UA supports different styles of creators, including gaming, chatting, co-hosting, events, and community-based LIVE content.

Are there forced diamond targets?

No. We encourage growth and consistency, but we do not believe in pressuring creators in a way that damages confidence or wellbeing.

Can I take a mental health break?

Yes. Communication matters, but creators are human. Breaks are respected and support is available through the correct channels.

Is UA only for UK creators?

UA is UK focused, but creators from Australia and New Zealand are welcome where support and onboarding are available.

Is there a cost to join?

No. UA is about creator support, not charging creators to be part of the community.
